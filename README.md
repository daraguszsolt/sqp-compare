# SQP Compare

A simple browser-based tool to compare Amazon Search Query Performance (SQP) reports across longer time periods than Amazon allows.

## What it does

Amazon's Brand Analytics only lets you compare SQP data within a single quarter. If you want to compare longer periods — for example, the first 5 months of 2025 vs the first 5 months of 2026 — you need to manually combine multiple reports.

This tool does that for you. Upload your SQP CSV exports, and it:

- Auto-detects the period each report covers
- Combines multiple reports into custom periods
- Shows a clean side-by-side comparison across all key metrics (search volume, impression share, click share, purchases)
- Works for both ASIN-level and Brand-level SQP reports
- Lets you filter, sort, and visualize the data

## How to use

1. Open the live tool: [https://daraguszsolt.github.io/sqp-compare/](https://daraguszsolt.github.io/sqp-compare/)
2. Export your SQP reports from Amazon Brand Analytics as CSV files
3. Upload them into the tool
4. Select your periods, apply filters, and compare

No installation. No data leaves your browser.

## Use cases

- Audits on new accounts
- Deep dives into a specific product or ASIN's organic traffic
- Tracking keyword-level market share over time
- Identifying long-term trends in impression share, click share, and purchase share
